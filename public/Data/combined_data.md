## Admission Requirements

### Undergraduate Requirements

**Gpa Requirement:** Combined GPA of 8.0 in SSC & HSC, minimum 3.5 in each

**O-Levels A-Levels:** Minimum average grade point, only one 'E' allowed

**Ib Diploma:** Minimum average 4.0/7.0

**Us High School:** Minimum 60%

**International:** 12th schooling year, min 60%, English/SAT/IELTS/TOEFL required

**Program Specific Requirements:** Stricter for Engg, Arch, Biochem, Microbiology, BPharm

**Test Waiver:** Possible with SAT, IELTS, TOEFL qualifying scores


### Graduate Requirements

**Program Specific Requirements:** Relevant undergraduate degree and grades, detailed per program

**Contact:** graduate admissions hotline/email for specifics


## Admission Process

1. Register with valid email/phone
2. Complete application form
3. Pay application fee
4. Print admit card for exam

## Admission Test

**Duration:** 2.5 hours

**Subjects:**

- English grammar
- Vocabulary
- Reading comprehension
- Essay
- Math

**Special Note:** 30 min drawing test for Architecture

**Instructions:** Bring admit card and ID, report 30 min before, no electronics allowed


## Documents Required Upon Admission

- SSC/O-levels, HSC/A-levels mark sheets/certificates
- Photos of candidate and parents
- NID/passport for candidate & parents

## Course Waiver

SAT/IELTS/TOEFL scores may waive ENG102 and specific courses (per policy)
